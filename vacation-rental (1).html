About the Stay

Your Private Coastal Haven

Nestled on a sun-kissed hillside overlooking the Aegean, Villa Serena is a thoughtfully designed retreat where understated luxury and natural beauty converge. Every detail — from the hand-laid stone floors to the panoramic sea-view terraces — has been curated with the discerning traveller in mind.

Whether you're lounging by the infinity pool, cooking a slow dinner in the fully-equipped gourmet kitchen, or watching the sun dip below the horizon with a glass of local wine, Villa Serena delivers an experience that feels both indulgent and effortlessly at home.
